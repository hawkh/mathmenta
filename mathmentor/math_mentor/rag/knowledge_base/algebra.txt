--- QUADRATIC EQUATIONS ---
Standard form: ax² + bx + c = 0
Quadratic formula: x = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / (2a)
Discriminant: D = b² - 4ac
  - D > 0: two distinct real roots
  - D = 0: one repeated real root
  - D < 0: two complex conjugate roots
Sum of roots: α + β = -b/a
Product of roots: αβ = c/a
Nature of roots guide: always compute D first

--- ALGEBRAIC IDENTITIES ---
(a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
(a - b)² = a² - 2ab + b²
(a + b)(a - b) = a² - b²
(a + b)³ = a³ + 3a²b + 3ab² + b³
(a - b)³ = a³ - 3a²b + 3ab² - b³
a³ + b³ = (a + b)(a² - ab + b²)
a³ - b³ = (a - b)(a² + ab + b²)
(a + b + c)² = a² + b² + c² + 2ab + 2bc + 2ca

--- PROGRESSIONS ---
Arithmetic Progression (AP):
  nth term: aₙ = a + (n-1)d
  Sum of n terms: Sₙ = n/2 × [2a + (n-1)d] = n/2 × (first + last)
  a = first term, d = common difference

Geometric Progression (GP):
  nth term: aₙ = a·rⁿ⁻¹
  Sum of n terms: Sₙ = a(rⁿ - 1)/(r - 1) for r ≠ 1
  Infinite GP sum (|r| < 1): S∞ = a/(1 - r)

Harmonic Progression (HP):
  Terms are reciprocals of an AP
  Harmonic mean H = 2ab/(a+b) for two terms a, b

Relation between means: AM ≥ GM ≥ HM (for positive numbers)
AM·HM = GM² (for two numbers)

--- BINOMIAL THEOREM ---
(a + b)ⁿ = Σₖ₌₀ⁿ C(n,k) · aⁿ⁻ᵏ · bᵏ
General term: Tₖ₊₁ = C(n,k) · aⁿ⁻ᵏ · bᵏ
Middle term: if n is even, middle term is T(n/2+1)
C(n,k) = n! / (k!(n-k)!)
Pascal's triangle rule: C(n,r) = C(n-1,r-1) + C(n-1,r)

--- INEQUALITIES ---
AM-GM: (a + b)/2 ≥ √(ab) for a, b > 0; equality when a = b
Cauchy-Schwarz: (a₁²+a₂²)(b₁²+b₂²) ≥ (a₁b₁+a₂b₂)²
Triangle inequality: |a + b| ≤ |a| + |b|

Solving quadratic inequalities:
  ax² + bx + c > 0: find roots α < β, then check sign in regions

--- COMPLEX NUMBERS ---
i = √(-1), i² = -1, i³ = -i, i⁴ = 1
z = a + bi; Re(z) = a, Im(z) = b
|z| = √(a² + b²) (modulus)
arg(z) = arctan(b/a) (argument, adjust for quadrant)
Conjugate: z̄ = a - bi
z · z̄ = |z|²
De Moivre: (cosθ + i sinθ)ⁿ = cos(nθ) + i sin(nθ)
Euler: e^(iθ) = cosθ + i sinθ

--- SETS & RELATIONS ---
|A ∪ B| = |A| + |B| - |A ∩ B|
|A ∪ B ∪ C| = |A| + |B| + |C| - |A∩B| - |B∩C| - |A∩C| + |A∩B∩C|

--- PERMUTATIONS & COMBINATIONS ---
Permutation: P(n,r) = n!/(n-r)!
Combination: C(n,r) = n!/(r!(n-r)!)
Circular permutation: (n-1)!
Permutation with repetition: n! / (n₁! n₂! ... nₖ!)

--- COMMON MISTAKES ---
- Don't forget ± in quadratic formula
- Check domain restrictions (denominators ≠ 0, square roots ≥ 0)
- When multiplying/dividing inequalities by negative numbers, flip the sign
- Verify both roots of quadratic actually satisfy the original equation (extraneous roots from squaring)
- In GP problems, check r ≠ 0 and r ≠ 1 before applying sum formula

--- SOLUTION STRATEGY TEMPLATES ---
For quadratic problems:
  1. Identify a, b, c from standard form
  2. Compute D = b² - 4ac
  3. Apply quadratic formula or factor
  4. Check discriminant for nature of roots

For inequality problems:
  1. Move all terms to one side
  2. Factor or complete the square
  3. Use sign chart (number line method)
  4. State answer as interval

For sequence/series problems:
  1. Identify type (AP/GP/HP)
  2. Find first term and common difference/ratio
  3. Apply appropriate formula
  4. Simplify carefully
